About Us

Belmont Station Merino & Possum is an online retailer specializing in high-quality merino and possum merino clothing products. As an additional income stream for Belmont Station, a large merino sheep farm in Mid Canterbury, New Zealand, the company offers a diverse range of products, including menswear, womenswear, children's clothing, accessories, blankets, yarn, and homewares.

Our collection features both our own branded items, made from wool sourced from merino sheep on the station, as well as products from other leading brands. Our inventory is carefully curated to showcase the best in merino and possum merino clothing, providing our customers with stylish and sustainable options.

At Belmont Station Merino & Possum, we pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer service and a seamless shopping experience. Our user-friendly website allows customers to browse our complete product catalog, filter products based on their preferences, and easily place orders.

We offer nationwide shipping in New Zealand, with a flat rate for standard-sized items and free shipping for orders over $200. International shipping is also available, with varying rates for different regions. Additionally, customers have the option to pick up their orders from Belmont Station.

We value customer loyalty and offer a points-based reward system, as well as regular promotions and specials. Our goal is to provide our customers with high-quality products, excellent service, and a rewarding shopping experience.

Explore our collection today and experience the luxury of merino and possum merino clothing from Belmont Station Merino & Possum.
